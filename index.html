VORTA

THE COMPLETE TOOLKIT FOR SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT

Vorta is powering one of the most fleksible and easy to use supply chain management tools on the market. Regardless of the size of your company we provide efficient and effordable tool that allows unique control of SCM chain. Vorta's meticulously designed user experience provides realtime insight in your business as well as detailed tracking of orders and shipment process. Vorta has been built in collaboration with SCM experts and as such has taken unique approach for problem solving in world of SCM.

FRESH APPROACH TO LOGISTICS

We believe that lack of good software for SCM is rooted in methadology, and not so much in software. Archaic approaches to these complex problems are omnipresent in the market and are instantly recognizible in way software is designed and organized. We obsessibely seek out elegant, composable abstractions which allow robust, adaptible solutions which are easy to undesrstand and follow. Because we eliminate needless complexity and extraneous details, our clients are able to focus on minimal set of information and thus do their job better.

ALWAYS BE IMPROVING

Vorta is an always-improving toolchain that gains new features every month. Our team is constantly working with our clients to face new challanges and provide elegant solutions which are in aligned with current Vorta structure. Our team includes quality and software engineers as well as SCM experts. Road is long and with every new step we take, we are constantly thinking on steps we took before and steps we will take in future. Some of our steps so far.





Supply Management

Adding new SKU(s) to system does not have to complicated process, and with Vorta it is not. In minutes user are able to add new products, define their characteristcs, set pricing and start the ordering process. Vorta allows planing of complex customer shipments. You need to prepare shipment and aggregate products from multiple inventories. We can handle that with suprising simplcity.

Encapusalted Access

Has the order been shipped yet? Have the customs cleared the shipment? Has customer been notified? Organization of this scale is always complicated endevour but it does not have to be. Vorta allows all participants access to the system and based on their permission access to needed information.

Planing & Shipment

Planing shipments is complicated endevour. Many goods have to be moved from internal locations before they can be sent to customers. Vorta handles this part of the process with ease. Plan your shipments in a such way where software will recognize future internal shipments and allow you to plan future customer shipments. Tracking on all the goods movements along the way.

Tracking

With Vorta you are able to track your product from manufacuring to end customer. With that information you can focus on which steps in your supply chain are having problems and have enough information to address them. Having issues with lost products and unexpected claims? Each product is uniquly tracked trough the system and detailed information is available in real-time.

Reporting

Since Vorta is controling all aspects of SCM of your company you are able to get details about each step of this way. Sales, factory availability, claim information etc. We as the company that recognizes importance of this type of information and are always ready to improve and add new reporting options.

Full Control

How many diffrent softwares are being used to organize logistic chain? How do you track shipments, orders, prices, claims, invoices, customs documents and list goes on. Vorta is designed and always improved to be the single piece of software you will need to manage your suplly chain.

METHODOLOGY & TECHNOLOGY

Using supply chain management can be needlessly complicated. That is for a good reason, many moving parts are hard to present to the user in a human readable way. That is why Vorta takes different approach. We avoid unnecessary complications that come with detailed customization of the software and instead we focus on optimal methodology for running supply chain management. This does not mean that the user cannot customize their experience, just the extent of customization is lowered so that user experience provide out of the box is best that it can be. By focusing on what is important we are able to streamline the process, providing a friendly interface that reduces possible mistakes with each step.
